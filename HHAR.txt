Activities including ‘Biking’, ‘Sitting’, ‘Standing’, ‘Walking’, ‘Stair Up’ and ‘Stair down’.
Sensors: Two embedded sensors such as Accelerometer and Gyroscope sampled at the highest frequency possible by the device.
Arrival_Time is the time when the measurement arrived to the sensing application.
Creation_Time is the time when the OS attaches to the sample.
Label: The activity that is performed by the user: bike sit, stand, walk, stairsup and stairsdown.
